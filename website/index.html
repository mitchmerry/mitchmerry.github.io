Brompton Folding Bicycles

M3L page S6R page P2L page

Brompton bikes are versatile and stylish bicycles for transportation and recreation. Because they fold, they can be taken places other bicycles can not.

Brompton bicycles are made in London and are used often by commuters because they can be taken aboard public transportation enabling multimodal means of commuting.

Brompton bicycles come in multiple different models that allow the rider to choose what attributes are important to them. The models are categorized by handlebar style, number of speeds, and the presence of racks or mudguards. Three of these models are below.

M3L

The M3L is a bike with 3 speeds, mudguards, and M style handlebars. Learn more about it here.

S6R

The S6R is a bike with 6 speeds, mudguards, a rack, and S style handlebars. Learn more about it here.

P2L

The P2L is a bike with 2 speeds, mudguards, and P style handlebars. Learn more about it here.
